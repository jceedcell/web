Fostering Innovation & Entrepreneurship

The Entrepreneur Development Cell (EDC) at Jain College of Engineering is a student-run organization dedicated to nurturing entrepreneurial spirit among students. We provide a platform for students to develop their business ideas, connect with mentors, and gain practical experience in entrepreneurship.

Our initiatives include workshops, speaker sessions, business plan competitions, and incubation support for promising startups. We collaborate with industry experts, alumni entrepreneurs, and venture capitalists to provide students with real-world insights.

Since our inception, we've helped launch over 50 student startups and have created a vibrant community of innovators and problem-solvers.

Our Services

We offer comprehensive support for aspiring entrepreneurs at every stage of their journey

Idea Validation

We help you test and validate your business ideas through market research and expert feedback before you invest time and resources.

Mentorship

Connect with experienced entrepreneurs, industry experts, and faculty members who can guide you through your entrepreneurial journey.

Workshops

Regular workshops on business planning, marketing, finance, legal aspects, and other essential entrepreneurial skills.

Competitions

Participate in business plan competitions and hackathons to test your ideas, win prizes, and get funding opportunities.

Networking

Access to a network of investors, alumni entrepreneurs, and like-minded peers to collaborate and grow together.

Incubation

Selected startups get access to our incubation center with workspace, resources, and seed funding opportunities.
